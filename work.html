Interactive Installation Unity TouchDesigner Video Mapping Cultural Design
Layered Identities
An interactive Portugal map installation surfacing the lived experiences of immigrant communities through contemporary traditional dress. Users interact with a map interface to explore 10 populations — each selection triggers a video projection onto a 3D figure dressed in clothing from that community's contemporary fashion landscape. Built with Unity for the interactive UI layer, TouchDesigner for real-time video processing and projection mapping, and a dataset of 300–400 curated, copyright-compliant clothing images.
Unity · TouchDesigner · Video Mapping · 3D Figure · 10 Communities · ~300–400 Images

Can UX Be Poetic?
An editorial UX essay exploring whether user experience design can carry emotional resonance without sacrificing clarity. Argues that the best interfaces — like the best poems — say exactly what they need to and nothing more. The page itself is the argument: sparse, deliberate, interactive.
