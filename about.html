Hana Morsi is a Learning Ops and AI Implementation Specialist with 8+ years leading technical programs across K–12 education and enterprise contexts. She has taught Computer Science, AI, Cybersecurity, and Web Development — including three years as Technical Program Coordinator in a Title-1 district — and currently delivers virtual CS and Engineering instruction nationally through Elevate K-12.

On the industry side, she consults on AI instructional projects, builds SCORM-compliant modules in Articulate 360, and manages LMS integration for enterprise clients. Currently completing a Master's in AI + Creative Computing at IADE University (2026–27), with a focus on ethical AI adoption and human-centered implementation.

Under the studio name hyasmo, she makes work that sits at the edge of systems and story — tools that teach, installations that ask questions, and interfaces that carry meaning.
